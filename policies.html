This Cookie Policy explains how Chingon Industries uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website or use our services. By using our services, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and account access.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.
  • Preference Cookies: Enable the website to remember information that changes the way the website behaves or looks, such as your preferred language or region.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track visitors across websites to allow us to display relevant advertisements.

3. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, if you limit the ability of websites to set cookies, you may worsen your overall user experience. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage or delete them, visit www.aboutcookies.org.
